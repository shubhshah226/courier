Company Profile



Parcel2Go was founded in the year 1976, with the sole aim of providing reliable and efficient door-to-door delivery service to businesses. Currently, Parcel2Go is one of the leading courier and cargo service providers in India. Venus's network spans across more than 1100 locations covering more than 12000 pin codes in India. A huge team of dedicated professionals deliver more than thousands of shipments on a daily basis.


Parcel2Go is currently expanding its operations rigorously in the Indian market. Parcel2Go is striving towards bringing some structure to this industry by providing superior customer service with the use of modern-day technology
